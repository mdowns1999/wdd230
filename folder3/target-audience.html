Target Audience

Personas 1: Jane Smith

Background:

Jane is a 21 year old woman that is called to serve a mission. Her family would like to travel to a temple that is special to their family. She would like all of her family to attend a temple session.

Jane's Goals:

  1. Serving a full-time mission
  2. Enjoying time with her family before she goes to serve.

Personas 2: John Hart

Background:

John is a 40 year old man with a family of four. His oldest son is getting married and John would like to find a reception area to celebrate his son's event.

John's Goals:

  1. Find a reception area big enough to house his family and friends
  2. Find a place where the whole family can stay the night.

Scenerio 1

How much is your room per night? What amenities are offered?

Scenerio 2

What does your reception area look like and what do other people think of it?
