Target Audience

The target Audience of the Rexburg Chamber of Commerce is business owners as well as local Rexburg residents. The goal of this site to to connect the businesses with other local owners. We also hope to make it to where residents will be aware of events going on in the community that might cause them to spend money and build the local economy.
