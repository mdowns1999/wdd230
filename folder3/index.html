Site Name: Temple Inn and Suites

Purpose:

The purpose of the Temple Inn and Suites is to provide a place for individuals to stay while they are waiting to attend the temple. It is also a place where individuals can hold events for their missionary farewells or wedding reception.

The site will list all the amenities that are offered. It will provide a contact/booking form if people want to reserve a room.
