Rexburg Demographics:

Rexburg has a population of about 39,000 people. The amount of poeple in Rexburg, however, grows dramatically due to the attendence of students that attend BYUI. Rexburg us continuing to grow and has new businesses moving into it each year. Rexburg grows about 2.38% each year. Rexburg also has a large poulation of LDS church members. they make up about 95% of the community.

Rexburg is the 5th largest city in Idaho. The age average is 25.2. The main reason for this is a religious school named BYU-I. The cost of living in lidaho is 1% lower than the national average. Only 7% of the population is unemployed and $31,000 is the average income.
