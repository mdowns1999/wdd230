About Provo City Center Temple

The Provo City Center Temple is one of the temples the Temple Inn and Suites provides transportation to. The temple was completed in the year 2016. Before this beutiful building became a temple, it was a tabernacle that served the community for several years. After a devestaing fire in 2010, President Thomas S. Monson, who was president of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints announced the remains of the building would be used in the construcition of a temple on the property.

Today the temple stands as a becon of hope and a blessing to many. Like other Latter-Day Saint temples, it provides its members with worship services and teaches them how to draw closer to God and his son Jesus Christ.
